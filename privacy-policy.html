Privacy Policy

Stellar Drift · com.myllkan.stellardrift · Effective date: July 8, 2026

Short version: Stellar Drift does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no network communication. Everything the game does happens on your device.

1. Who we are

Stellar Drift is a single-player space combat game developed and published by an independent developer ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the game handles — which is, deliberately, almost nothing.

2. Data we collect

None. Stellar Drift does not collect any personal information. Specifically, the game does not:

  • require or offer user accounts or sign-in
  • collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data
  • use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking tools
  • show advertisements or contain third-party ad SDKs
  • transmit any data over the internet

3. Data stored on your device

The game saves a small amount of gameplay state locally on your device (for example, whether you have already watched an in-game cutscene, so it is not replayed). This data never leaves your device, contains no personal information, and is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear its storage.

4. Permissions

Stellar Drift does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, files, or any other sensitive device features.

5. Third-party services

The game itself integrates no third-party services. It is distributed through Google Play, which is operated by Google and governed by Google's own privacy policy. Any data Google collects in connection with downloading or updating the app (such as install statistics) is handled by Google, not by us.

6. Children

Because Stellar Drift collects no data from anyone, it also collects no data from children. The game is a general-audience title and does not knowingly gather personal information from users of any age.

7. Changes to this policy

If a future update ever changes what data the game handles (for example, adding online features), this policy will be updated first and the effective date above revised. The current version is always available at this address.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to myllkan2@gmail.com.
